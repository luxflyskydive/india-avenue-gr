Our Story

About India Avenue

A labor of love rooted in the subcontinent's oldest culinary traditions, brought to Greenwich.

Where It All Began

A vision born from passion for Indian cuisine

India Avenue takes you on a culinary adventure to the farthest corners of India and allows you to taste the finest in Indian cuisine. The ambience of the regal courts is carefully crafted through majestic paintings and warm ornaments of that era.

The rich ambience only enriches the flavour of the wonderful array of exotic Indian delicacies brought to your table by our gracious staff. Our glass-panelled tandoor kitchen with clay oven enables our patrons to have a rare look into the impeccable creativity behind our chefs' sumptuous dishes.

The owner of India Avenue has hand-picked these chefs to masterfully create a menu that captures the flavour of the Indian subcontinent. Fragrant and warm spices from the four corners of the country are delicately blended in meticulous proportions to create the dishes we present to you.

The epicure will find that our culinary inspirations re-design a variety of South Asian cuisines with a studied precision for the North American palate with a wonderfully sensual result. Tender morsels of meat, an array of kebabs, piquant curries and an assortment of breads and pilafs leave our patrons looking forward to their next visit.

The Glass Tandoor Kitchen

A window into our craft

One of the signature features of India Avenue is our glass-panelled tandoor kitchen. Visible from the dining room, it gives our guests a rare and intimate look at the craft behind the food — the clay oven glowing with heat, the dough being stretched and slapped against its walls, the skewers of seasoned meats emerging with that unmistakable char.

The tandoor is the heart of our kitchen. Our naans, breads, and tandoori dishes are all prepared here, to order, every time.
