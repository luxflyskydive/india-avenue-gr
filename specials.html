Lunch Specials

A Greenwich tradition, weekdays only

Monday – Friday

Our lunch specials are one of the most beloved things we do. A complete, warm Indian meal that's become a fixture for working professionals and regulars across Greenwich. You get the same care in preparation that goes into our dinner service, just in a format built for the midday hour.

Our lunch menu features a full selection of curries, kathi rolls, and Indo-Chinese dishes — all served at lunch prices with rice and naan. Choose from vegetarian, chicken, lamb, and seafood options every weekday.
