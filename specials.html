Lunch Specials

A Greenwich tradition, weekdays only

Monday – Friday

Our lunch specials are one of the most beloved things we do. A complete, warm Indian meal — served at an honest price — that's become a fixture for working professionals and regulars across Greenwich. You get the same care in preparation that goes into our dinner service, just in a format built for the midday hour.

Our lunch menu features a full selection of curries, kathi rolls, and Indo-Chinese dishes — all served at lunch prices with rice and naan. Choose from vegetarian, chicken, lamb, and seafood options every weekday.

Private Dining

For groups that deserve more

India Avenue is available for private dining events, corporate lunches, and celebratory dinners. Whether it's a birthday, an anniversary, a business dinner, or a cocktail reception with passed appetizers, we can accommodate groups with a custom experience.

Menu Highlights

A few of our most beloved dishes

✦ ✦ ✦
Spring
Lamb Rogan Josh

Slow-braised Kashmiri lamb with whole spices, a signature of the spring menu. Rich, aromatic, and intensely satisfying after the cold months.

Summer
Mango Lassi & Shrimp Coconut Curry

Chilled mango lassi made with Alphonso pulp, paired with a light coconut shrimp curry from Kerala. Bright, cooling, perfect for warm evenings.

Autumn
Butternut Squash Dal

A seasonal take on our slow-cooked lentil dal, enriched with roasted butternut squash and warming spices. Earthy, grounding, and deeply comforting.
