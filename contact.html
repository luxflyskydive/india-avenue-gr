Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you call

Do you take reservations?
Yes — we recommend calling ahead, especially for weekend dinners and groups of 4 or more. We accept reservations by phone at +1 (203) 717-1567. Walk-ins are always welcome, though wait times may apply on busy nights.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?
Absolutely. We have a wide selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes, and many of our dishes can be adjusted for gluten sensitivity. Please let your server know about any allergies when you arrive, or mention them in your reservation so we can prepare. We take allergies seriously.
Do you offer catering or private dining?
Yes. We cater events of all sizes and host private dining experiences for groups. Whether you're planning a corporate lunch, a birthday dinner, or a full buyout, reach out via the form above or call us and we'll put together something tailored to your event.
How spicy is the food?
Spice levels are adjustable for virtually every dish. Our kitchen cooks to your preference — just let your server know whether you prefer mild, medium, or you want the full heat. We never use spice as a shortcut; every level is made with the same care.
Is there parking nearby?
Yes. Street parking is available on Railroad Ave and surrounding streets. The Arch Street public parking lot is about a one-minute walk from the restaurant. On weekday lunches, street parking is usually available right out front.
Do you offer takeout and online ordering?
Yes — we use Toast for online ordering. You can place your order ahead of time and pick it up at your convenience. Visit our Toast page or tap "Order Online" in the navigation.
