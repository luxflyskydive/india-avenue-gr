Getting Here

By car, train, or on foot

🚗
By Car

We're located on Railroad Ave in downtown Greenwich, easily accessible from I-95 (Exit 3) or the Merritt Parkway (Exit 31). Street parking is available on Railroad Ave and surrounding streets. A public parking lot is located one block away on Arch Street.

🚆
By Train

India Avenue is a short walk from Greenwich Train Station on the Metro-North New Haven Line. From the station, head south on Greenwich Ave, then right on Railroad Ave — we're about a 5-minute walk. Perfect for a dinner trip from the city.

🚶
On Foot

If you're already in downtown Greenwich, we're just off the main strip. From Greenwich Avenue, head east on Railroad Ave and look for our sign. The building has warm lighting and the tandoor kitchen is often visible from the sidewalk on cooler nights.
